6 Days Rwanda Wildlife & Forest Safari

6 Days / 5 Nights · From $3,200 / person

Beyond gorillas - Big Five game drives in restored Akagera National Park and chimpanzee tracking with canopy walkway adventures in ancient Nyungwe Forest.

Activities: Game drives, Boat safari, Chimpanzee tracking, Canopy walk

Parks visited: Akagera National Park, Nyungwe National Park

Package Highlights

  • Big Five savannah safari
  • Ancient rainforest trek
  • Canopy walkway included
  • Rwanda's two contrasting parks
  • Private guide and vehicle

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Kigali to Akagera

Drive east to Akagera - afternoon game drive.

Day 2

Full day Akagera

Morning and afternoon drives - lion, elephant and Lake Ihema boat trip.

Day 3

Akagera to Nyungwe

Cross Rwanda to the southern rainforest.

Day 4

Chimpanzee tracking

Morning chimp trek in Nyungwe Forest.

Day 5

Canopy walkway

Canopy walk and waterfall trail - colobus monkey viewing.

Day 6

Return to Kigali

Scenic drive north - Kigali departure.
