Rwanda Safaris

East Africa · Gorillas, rainforest & Lake Kivu

Rwanda - the Land of a Thousand Hills - packs extraordinary biodiversity and culture into one of Africa's most compact and welcoming nations. From misty volcanoes to savannah plains and ancient rainforests, every landscape tells a story of conservation and renewal.

Volcanoes National Park made Rwanda synonymous with mountain gorilla tracking, while Akagera's restored lion and rhino populations bring the Big Five within reach. Nyungwe Forest shelters chimpanzees and a world-class canopy walkway.

Charming towns along Lake Kivu and the immaculate capital Kigali add urban sophistication to any itinerary - making Rwanda the perfect complement to a Tanzania or Uganda safari.

Safari Highlights

  • Mountain Gorilla Tracking - Volcanoes National Park in the Virunga Mountains
  • Big Five Safari - Akagera National Park savannah and wetlands
  • Nyungwe Forest - chimpanzees, canopy walkway and 300+ bird species
  • Lake Kivu - lakeside relaxation in Rubavu and Karongi
  • Kigali - clean, safe capital with museums and vibrant dining
