Uganda Safaris

East Africa · Gorillas, savannah & Nile adventures

Welcome to Uganda, the Pearl of Africa, where breathtaking landscapes and vibrant wildlife create an unforgettable adventure. National parks are sanctuaries of biodiversity showcasing stunning natural beauty and rich ecosystems.

From the misty mountains of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, home to majestic mountain gorillas, to the expansive savannahs of Murchison Falls where the Nile thunders over dramatic waterfalls, each park offers a unique experience. Uganda is a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts, bird watchers and nature lovers.

Uganda's cities blend traditional African culture with modern influences - from bustling Kampala to adventure-filled Jinja at the source of the Nile - serving as gateways to gorilla trekking, wildlife safaris and lakeside relaxation.

Safari Highlights

  • Mountain Gorilla Trekking - half the world's population in Bwindi and Mgahinga
  • Murchison Falls - the Nile plunging through a narrow gorge
  • Chimpanzee Tracking - Kibale Forest, the primate capital of East Africa
  • Queen Elizabeth - tree-climbing lions and Kazinga Channel boat cruises
  • Adventure Capital - white-water rafting and bungee jumping in Jinja
