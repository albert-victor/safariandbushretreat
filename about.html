About Safari & Bush Retreats

Real guides · Real bush · No middlemen

Safari and Bush Retreats is a fully licensed Tanzanian tour operator serving travellers across the entire country - from the Serengeti and Ngorongoro in the north to Ruaha and Nyerere in the south, Katavi and Mahale in the west, and Zanzibar's Indian Ocean shores.

We are not tied to a single region. Our guides, drivers and planners operate on every major circuit, with deep knowledge of wildlife seasons, park regulations, lodge partnerships and the quiet roads that make a safari feel effortless.

Led by certified guide Ally Shebe, we design bespoke journeys without agents or mark-ups in between - direct, honest and shaped around how you want to experience Africa.

Our Team

People who know every road

Behind every itinerary is a close-knit team of professional safari guides, experienced drivers, reservation specialists and on-ground coordinators - all Tanzanian, all passionate about wildlife and hospitality.

Ally Shebe, our lead guide and founder, holds national certification and years of experience across all circuits. He trains and works alongside a roster of specialists: northern migration trackers, southern wilderness experts, mountain trek leaders and coastal hosts who know Zanzibar's tides and traditions.

Whether you fly into Kilimanjaro, Dar es Salaam or Zanzibar, we meet you where you are and build a route that flows naturally - no backtracking, no generic packages.

  • Certified safari & mountain guides
  • English-speaking drivers on all circuits
  • 24-hour guest support while on safari
  • Direct booking - no overseas middlemen
