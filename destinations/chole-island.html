Chole Island

Heritage · Mafia Island · 1 tour

Chole Island, once at a crossroads of a major trade route is now considered a place where one can take a glance back in time. Chole Island is one of the smallest of the islands that comprise the Mafia Archiepelago and is located just across the channel from us. Chole is around one kilometre long and half a kilometre wide and bursting at the seams with nature.
