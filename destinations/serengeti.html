Northern Circuit UNESCO

Serengeti National Park

National Park · 14,763 km² · Jun - Oct peak · 24 tours

Serengeti National Park is unequalled for its natural beauty and scientific value - the greatest concentration of plains game in Africa across nearly 15,000 km² of rolling savanna.

Established in 1952, the park hosts the world-famous Great Migration of over two million wildebeest and zebra, followed by lion, cheetah and crocodile along the Mara and Grumeti rivers.

The ecosystem divides into three sections: the central Seronera plains, the western Grumeti corridor and the remote northern Lobo area bordering Kenya's Maasai Mara.

Best time to visit: Jun - Oct for migration in north; Jan - Feb for calving in south; year-round wildlife

Recommended duration: 2 - 5 days recommended

Entry cost: Park fees from approx. $70 / adult / day (plus concession fees)

Key Attractions

  • Great Wildebeest Migration
  • Seronera Valley big cats
  • Grumeti & Mara river crossings
  • Balloon safaris at dawn
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site

Activities

  • Game drives
  • Hot air balloon safari
  • Great Migration tracking
  • Bird watching
  • Photography safaris
