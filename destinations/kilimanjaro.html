Northern Circuit 5,895 m

Mount Kilimanjaro

National Park · 1,668 km² · Climbing seasons · 30 tours

Mount Kilimanjaro rises to 5,896 metres above northern Tanzania - Africa's highest peak and the world's tallest free-standing mountain.

Climbers pass through rainforest, moorland, alpine desert and glacier zones without technical climbing equipment on routes including Machame, Lemosho, Marangu and Rongai.

The mountain's ecosystems support elephant, leopard, buffalo and endemic species in the montane forest belt below the summit.

Best time to visit: Jan - Feb and Jul - Sep (avoid long rains Mar - May)

Recommended duration: 5 - 9 days trekking depending on route

Entry cost: Park & camping fees from approx. $800 - $1,200+ per trek (route dependent)

Key Attractions

  • Uhuru Peak (5,895 m)
  • Machame & Lemosho routes
  • Shira Plateau
  • Kibo crater
  • Rainforest to alpine desert zones

Activities

  • Mountain trekking
  • Day hikes (Shira Plateau)
  • Photography
  • Wildlife in montane forest
  • Summit certificate trek
