Nyerere National Park

National Park · Former Selous · Rufiji River · 17 tours

Nyerere National Park - formerly the Selous Game Reserve - is Africa's largest protected area, centred on the Rufiji River delta in southern Tanzania.

Boat safaris through hippo and crocodile channels, guided walking trails and wild dog tracking offer a safari experience unlike the northern parks.

Fly-in camps and the Beho Beho luxury lodge serve discerning travellers seeking remote wilderness.

Best time to visit: Jun - Oct dry season; river concentrations peak Jul - Sep

Recommended duration: 3 - 5 days recommended

Entry cost: Park fees from approx. $50 / adult / day

Key Attractions

  • Rufiji River boat safaris
  • Walking safaris
  • Wild dog packs
  • Hippo & crocodile channels
  • Fly-in remote camps

Activities

  • Boat safaris
  • Game drives
  • Walking safaris
  • Fly camping
  • Bird watching
